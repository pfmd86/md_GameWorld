👨‍👩‍👧 For Parents

🎮 About the Games

These free educational games are designed to help young children (ages 4–8) practise essential early learning skills in a fun, distraction-free environment:

  • 🧮 Math — Addition, subtraction & number comparison
  • 🇩🇪 Deutsch — German vocabulary with pictures & audio
  • ⏰ Tell the Time — Reading analog clocks at 4 difficulty levels

🔒 Privacy & Data

Your child's privacy is fully protected:

  • All games run entirely in the browser — no server, no account
  • No personal data is collected while playing
  • High scores are saved only in your browser's local storage and never leave the device
  • No ads, no trackers, no third-party analytics
  • No cookies beyond local storage for scores

If these games bring a smile to your child's face, a small donation helps keep them free and ad-free for everyone!
